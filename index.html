Who We Are?

Sanabal Al Shamal was established by Chairman Mubarak AL Mehazaa along with Mr. Mohammad Sarowar Mamur, partner and managing director. The promoters believed in the tremendous potential of the state of Qatar and the opportunities available in the field of transportation and road conveyance. Sanabal Al Shamal of the well diversified density group of companies, having interest in Transportation, swing and Manpower. Located in central Doha, the group is poised for major expansion in the coming years.
